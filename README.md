#  Advanced eCommerce Management System with Integrated Logistics & Marketing

##  Overview

This project is a **modern, beginner-friendly SaaS-based eCommerce platform** designed to simplify online business management. It integrates **store creation, logistics, AI-based risk analysis, and marketing tools** into a single unified dashboard.

Unlike traditional platforms like Shopify or WooCommerce, this system focuses on:

* Simplicity for beginners
* AI-powered decision making
* Unified logistics and marketing

---

## Objectives

* Build a **user-friendly eCommerce platform** for beginners
* Provide **AI-based customer risk scoring**
* Integrate **multiple courier services in one system**
* Enable **smart marketing campaign management**
* Deliver a **centralized dashboard** for complete store control

---

## Target Users

* New eCommerce entrepreneurs
* Small business owners
* Sellers looking for an all-in-one solution

---

##  Key Features

###  Store Builder

* 20+ ready-made themes
* Drag & drop customization
* One-click store publishing

### One-Click Product Import

* Import products from any website
* Auto-fetch title, images, price, and description

###  AI Customer Risk System

* Tracks:

  * Fake orders
  * Returns
  * Cancellations
* Assigns risk score to each customer

### Smart Logistics System

* Multi-courier integration
* Compare shipping rates
* Print labels & track shipments

###  AI Courier Recommendation

* Suggests best courier based on:

  * City performance
  * Delivery success rate
  * Cost vs speed

### Payment Integration

* Stripe, PayPal, and local gateways
* Multi-currency support

### Marketing Integration

* Meta Ads & TikTok Ads dashboard
* Control campaigns directly

###  Central Dashboard

* Manage:

  * Products
  * Orders
  * Customers
  * Shipments
  * Ads
